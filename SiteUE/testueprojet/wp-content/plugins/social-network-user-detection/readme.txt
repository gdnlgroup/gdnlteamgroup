Detects the login status of social media users and records to your site's Google Analytics. (Currently supports Facebook, Twitter, Google, Google+)

== Description ==

Have you ever wondered what social networks your site visitors are using at the time they're using your site? Social Media User Detection by <a href="http://adaptpartners.com/">Adapt Partners</a> is the answer.

Our useful plugin facilitates the detection and logging of your site visitors' social media login status.

By knowing which social media sites your users are logged into at the time they visit your site, you can prioritize your social marketing campaigns, presentation of social media voting buttons, and more. Knowledge is power. Now you have it.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the downloaded plugin file `social-media-user-detection.zip`,
2. Upload the `social-media-user-detection` the to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory,
3. Activate the plugin "Social Media User Detection" through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress,
4. That's it!  You will now see a new menu item under "Settings" called "User Detection".  Follow the instructions at http://adaptpartners.com/wordpress/social-media-user-detection/ to setup your Facebook App and Custom Segments in Google Analytics (easy) and you're ready to go!
